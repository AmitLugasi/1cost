Privacy Policy

At 1Cost, your financial privacy is our top priority. We follow a strict "No Data Collection" policy.

  • ☁️ iCloud Sync: Data is synced only through your personal Apple iCloud account.
  • 🚫 No Tracking: We do not use third-party trackers or sell your information.
