Privacy Policy for 1Cost

Last Updated: March 2026

At 1Cost, your privacy is our top priority. As a security-focused application, we operate on the principle of Zero Data Collection.

1. Data Storage

All financial data, expense titles, and category information are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData technology. We do not have access to your data, and we do not use any external servers to store or process your financial information.

2. Location Data (Spending Map)

1Cost offers a "Spending Map" feature that allows you to visualize where your purchases occurred.

  • Collection: If you enable Location Tracking in Settings, the app will capture your GPS coordinates at the time of an expense.
  • Storage: This location data is stored exclusively on your device.
  • Usage: Coordinates are used only to render "drops" on your personal map. We never share, sell, or transmit your location data to third parties or external servers.

3. Apple Pay & Shortcuts Integration

1Cost integrates with iOS Shortcuts to automate expense logging. This integration happens locally on your iPhone. The app only processes the data provided by the shortcut you've configured.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising frameworks. Your financial habits remain private to you.

5. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this policy, you can contact Amit Lugasi via the app's GitHub repository.
